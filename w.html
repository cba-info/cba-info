Calculate Your BMR and TDEE

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions.

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is the number of calories you need to maintain your current weight, based on your activity level.
